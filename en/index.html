The Begining

Intech LLC was founded in 2004 by Diamond Systems for its sales and customer service in Latin America, and to provide complementary equipment from other companies.

Febuary 2007

Acquisition

In 2007 DiamondSystems was acquired by MobaNL. IntechLLC was not included in the MobaNL acquisition.

Febuary 2008

Reovotec was Founded

In 2008 IntechLLC was wholly acquired from TalonLLC (former owner of Diamond) by the current group. The group that owns IntechLLC founded Reovotec S.A in 2008. In Guadalajara, Mexico. Several Engineers and former DiamondSystems employees make up the new team. The low cost of labor and operation in Mexico allows us to offer a fairly competitive price without compromising quality.
